About Us

OUR PHILOSOPHY

What Aslan isn’t: another startup content to be modest in our ambitions, transactional in our motivations, and fair-weathered on our commitment to this problem. This isn’t an exercise to amass riches, notoriety, or status. We are not mercenaries.
What Aslan is: an inherently gutsy bid to push the boundaries of what is knowable (and when) in an effort to end the most elusive forms of violence and harm. An impact to be made and felt over the horizon of decades, not years (or quarters). We are missionaries before we are anything else.
